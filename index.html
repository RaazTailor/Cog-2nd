Policy

This research takes your 15 to 20 minutes. We ensure that the personal Information and data will be kept confidential.

Instructions

This survey contains 16 questions: For two categories

  1. Time free questions
  2. Time limit questions

Both categories of questions will come randomly and in time bound questions you must take decision or select your option within time-limit. Once the time is up, the next question will appear automatically.

After each answered question, you will be asked to rate your confidence on decision using a Likert scale:

1=Least Confidence, 5= Fully Confidence
